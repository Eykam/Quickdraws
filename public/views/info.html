Quickdraw

A pistol dueling game where players can compete against their friends online

  • On round start players spawn in the center of the map facing each other
  • A randomized timer is set between 5-8 seconds
  • When timer is started, players turn and walk automatically
  • At the end of the randomized timer, players will turn to face each other and shooting is enabled
  • To shoot players can use any key
  • First player to shoot after turning wins the round
  • First player to win 3 rounds wins the game
